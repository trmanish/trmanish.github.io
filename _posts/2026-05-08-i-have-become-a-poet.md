<div align="center">
  <h1><strong>I Have Become a Poet</strong></h1>
</div>

<br>

<div align="center" style="font-style: italic; color: #555;">
  for the men who never said it,<br>
  and the women who deserved to hear it
</div>

<br>

<div align="center">

The men who never spoke<br>
are the ones writing now.<br>
The ones who held the words back<br>
like a glass too full to lift,<br>
they are the poets.<br>

<br>

I never thought I'd be one.

</div>

<br>

<div align="center">

You walked into a city<br>
that wasn't yours<br>
and somehow,<br>
it became yours.<br>
A kiss in a car<br>
I did not know I had in me.<br>
Twenty more minutes,<br>
I asked the road.<br>
The road said no.

</div>

<br>

<div align="center" style="font-style: italic; color: #444;">

You are the kind of beautiful<br>
that makes a man forget<br>
he was ever quiet.<br>

<br>

You feel like a rose<br>
that bloomed in a chest<br>
that did not know<br>
it had room for a garden.<br>

<br>

You feel like a quiet morning<br>
someone else is having,<br>
a song I have stopped singing<br>
but cannot stop humming.<br>

<br>

And by the way,<br>
I have been walking<br>
through a February rain<br>
for years now,<br>
and it still has not ended<br>
in my mind.

</div>

<br>

<div align="center">

I told myself I was holding back the storm.<br>
The storm was inside me the whole time,<br>
moving every mountain<br>
life threw at it,<br>
except the one<br>
that mattered.<br>

<br>

The poet drowns.<br>
That is what poets do.<br>
It is innate.<br>
And I drowned in the silence<br>
between two people<br>
who never quite said the word<br>
that would have changed everything.

</div>

<br>

<div align="center" style="font-style: italic; color: #444;">

You feel like a rose.<br>
And the chest you bloomed in<br>
still keeps the garden.<br>
Even when you do not.<br>
Even when I will not say it<br>
out loud,<br>
to anyone,<br>
ever again.

</div>
